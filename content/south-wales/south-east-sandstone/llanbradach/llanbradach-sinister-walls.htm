1. Sinister (7a+) ** superb overhanging climbing with long reach at start. Classic Rebolted

2. Abbattoir and Costello (7b+) *** the centre of the black wall. Intricate, sustained, steep and brilliant. Rebolted

3. In Blood, Of Life, Of Sin ? (7a+) ** excellent fingery wall climbing with a dynamic crux.

4. Evil Eye (7a) * cracks and wall past 'eye' to a difficult slab. Good climbing, sometimes wet but still climbable.

5. Letters of Life (6c) * left side of blunt arete. One difficult move. Also gives access to the ledge for routes above. Rebolted

6. Dreaming in Colour (7a) ** superb sustained and fingery wall climbing. Crux at start but no respite after that. Rebolted

7. Giving it all Up (7a) ** the centre of the wall. Desperate moves at half-height. Rebolted

8. The Host (6c) * wall just left of shallow groove utilizing flaky holds. Finish on left. Rebolted

9. Food for Parasites (7a+) ** fine wall with another difficult mid-height crux. Fingery. Rebolted

10. Bringeth yon Leach (7a+) * left-hand side of face. Heinous crimping.

11. Snapper (6c) straightforward climbing in a superb position. Two hard moves.

12. Overleaf (6b) headwall above left-hand wall. Gained from Letters of Life.
