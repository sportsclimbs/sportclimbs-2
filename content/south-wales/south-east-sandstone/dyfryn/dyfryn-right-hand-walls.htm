The walls to the right are a little more complex but the following list of routes should help:

22 Off to Oz (5 ) the short wall complete with two peg runners

23 Ed's Triumph (5+) slightly harder to the right to a sling and bolt lower off.

24 Nick's Dilemma (6a) a better route via the pleasant face to the right.

25 Playing Away (6a+)

26 A Bit of Nokia Side (6b) a rounded pillar with staples

27 Barbara (6b) the steep shallow groove

28 Milky White (6a) the second groove line passing a bed knob

29 Why did I bother (6b+) a fingery wall with hidden holds

30 Predictive Text (6b) ★ an excellent short steep wall

31 Baggle Brook Affair (5) a nice slab climb and passing large pocket in wall above

32 Nifty Fingers (6a) wall, ledges and headwall above flake.

33 Lip Service (6a) wall with bed-knob and steep headwall.

34 Family Day (6b) ★ overlap with steep headwall.

35 Affairs of Man (6b) the corner with a steep headwall.

36 Supply on Demand (6b) Steep wall on pockets to lower off of next route.

37 Demolition Gang (6a) Groove left of birch tree of next route.

38 Birthday Bulge (5) initial tricky wall passing silver birch .Scrap metal galore marks the line

39 Project -Ed's Folly (6a) nice wall behind Elder tree

40 Memory Man (6b) bridge up chimney trend up left via secret hold.

41 Gathering Gloom (6a+) Wall right of chimney with a long reach to finish

42 Ockers delight (6b) Start at bulging nose and go up wall to a fine finish over the overhang.

43 Nick to the Rescue (6c) ★ Wall above cave to juggy finale.
