The slabby section of crag to the left of Violation Sector is a complex series of slabs with some good introduction routes.

1. Take the Wave (6a+) right out of cave then pleasant wall

2. Genetic Engineering (HVS 5a) * straight over bulge then face and diagonal crack

3. Genetically Engineered Super Mutant (VS 4b) left from cave then groove

4. A Liberal Smear (6a+) face and groove with difficulties low down

5. Get Them Out by Xmas (6b+) short problem via faint crack to tree

6. Nation of Shopkeepers (6a+) rightwards via slabby wall

7. The Microwave Man (6b) direct just to left

8. Kalamazoo (E1 6a) short test-piece. Overgrown.
