A pleasant face to tricky exit.

**4. The Desperation of Freedonia (6a)**  
Slabby wall to ledge and steep finish all right of degenerating groove.

**5. The Reincarnation of Catatonia (6a)**

**10. The One in Ten (6a+) *★***
