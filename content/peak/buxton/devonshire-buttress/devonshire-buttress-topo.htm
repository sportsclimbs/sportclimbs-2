1. Men are From Mars, Women are from Venus (6c) bouldery scoop.

2. Dripping Mercury (6c+) desperate wall to left

3. It's Uranus (7a) harder wall left again.

4. Idiot Nation (6c) shallow crack and open groove. Tough.

5. Savage Girth (6b+) ★ tricky moves into shallow groove. Finish direct.

6. The Age of Reason (6b) ★ shallow groove and wall. Pleasant

7. Clotted Cream (6a) ★ pleasant gentle face climbing.

8. Little Brown Men (6c) more technical to the left. Awkward crux.

9. We Are not Alone (6c) slabby wall and bulge with long reach.

10. Here (7a) ★ fingery and technical face to left

11. Jihad (7b) ★ thin and technical up the centre of the bald face. Blind.

12. Jewfish (7b) ★ short desperate to left joining the next route.

13. Chain Reaction (6c+) blunt rib and undercut through left side of arch

14. Last Man First (7a) ★ technical start and excellent upper section.

15. Infantada (6b+) ★ blunt rib to and through right side of bulge.

16. And the Roof Fell in (7b) difficult roof. Take care with rock.

17. Gimme Shelter (7a) centre of overlap. Good moves.

18. Nice Melons (6b) rib and short bulge.

Banarama (6b+) right-hand of two short lines on left of crag.

The Grape and Rye (6b+) left-hand and slightly better line
