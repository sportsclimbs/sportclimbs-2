From below the right-hand end of Cairn Sector (Breakfast at Safeways etc.) drop down below the concrete posts and turn left to reach this very pleasant face. Towards its left-hand side are four relatively short routes.

1. Ashford and Sons (6a), 2. 4Ben (5+), 3. By Caesarian (5), 4. New Arrivals (6a), 5. Expecting (5), 6. Induction Program(5), 7. Premature (6a) 8. unnamed (6a)

6. Safe Haven (5) easier of two left-hand lines

7. The Hollow Man (5) direct line with hollow flake. New belay added.

8. Christian Salvage Man (6a) ★ pleasant face, named after local security man! Rebolted and new belay added.

9. Which Depp-Artment (6a) via bulge and then rightwards to belay. Rebolted, new low bolt and new belay added.

10. Ichabod (5+) awkward finishing bulge. Rebolted, new low bolt and new belay added.

11. Haven sent (6a) new

12. Sleepy Hollow (6a) ★ fine climbing via faint rib. Rebolted and new belay added.

13. Bonny Helena (5) squeezed in but good just to the right. Rebolted and new belay added.

14. Gone for a Tim Burton (5) just left of obvious flake/corner. Rebolted and new belay added.

15. The Height Below (5) wall via brown streak. Rebolted and new belay added.

16. The Sanctuarian (6c) hard through overlap with tricky wall below. Rebolted and new belay added.

17. For Haven's Sake (6b) ★ right side of overlap. Rebolted and new belay added.

18. Downtown (6b) ★ excellent shallow groove. Slightly bold. Rebolted and new belay added.

19. What Lies Beneath (6b) ★ tricky wall to finish. Rebolted and new belay added.

20. Haven Blessed (6a+) new

21. Haven or Hell (6a+) pillar at right-hand end of wall.
