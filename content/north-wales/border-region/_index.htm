The North Wales region provides a wealth of sport climbing in many different styles, difficulty levels and even rock types. Currently we only provide topos to a limited range of the climbing here. The border region includes the vast area of Clwyd limestone, Devil's Gorge and Llanymynech. The North Coast region currently only covers the unusual climbing of The Gallery. Hopefully more crags will be included in future updates.
