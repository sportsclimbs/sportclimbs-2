Taffs Well North Wall

A very fine wall that has suffered the ignominy of misuse. Compact, technical face climbing abounds and all the routes are worthwhile. Can suffer dampness after rain and in humidity.

1. The Creaming Dream (6c+) * a good intro to the wall. Slabby and technical

2. Ice Cream Sunday (7a) * very technical climbing with a stubborn finish. Hard.

3. Scream for Cream (7a+) * thin and technical at the start, quite vicious really. Needs rebolting.

GLC Safeasfuck (7b) * vague line from inset corner low down

4. Trailblazer (7b) ** in many ways the route of the wall. Sustained and technical with a difficult rightwards finish.

5. Sink or Swim (7a+) * sustained and airy climbing with a blind finish. Gold BRs.

6. Security Plus (7b) * strenuous climbing on undercut holds. An easy start but difficult and complicated above.

7. Give it Some Belly (7b) * new line with a desperate crux through the bulge and awkward finale.

8. Norman Normal (7a) * bold start and hard finish. Just merits a French grade, worthwhile all the same. To be rebolted. Norman was never nothing more than normal!

9. Give it Some Wellie (6c) shallow scoop/groove above overlap to belay.

10. All's Well (6b) * the warm up for the wall giving a very pleasant pitch.

11. The Bitter End (6b+) squeezed in onto the end of the wall. A tough crux.

Adam Hussein's An (6b) rightmost line of wall gained from mossy slab.

13. Taffy Duck E3 6a ** the diagonal crackline across the wall starting from All's Well. As good as any on the wall. Mostly situ gear.

Affluence (5+) leftmost of lines over easier ground

Honeysuckle Supreme (5) the line of huge bolts

Faster Pussycat (5+) from a tree stump climb past a ledge and up a leaning wall.

14. Tinker's Dog (6a) the left-hand route is pleasantly sustained

15. Full Metal Jacket (6b) steep finale to finish at ledge.

16. Any Old Iron (6c) pleasant face to steep finale.

17, Rag and Bone (6b) pleasant face and steep finale.

Clarts Free Taxi Ride to Visit Madam Hussein (5) diagonal line clipping every bolt imaginable

Wrecker's Ball (6a) face finishing atop a large block.

18. Knackers Yard (6b+) right again finishing up crack. Needs regearing.

Landfill Tax (6a) short face to lower off on 19.

19. Sustainable Development (5+) pleasant face to steep finale.
